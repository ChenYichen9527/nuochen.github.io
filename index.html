Brief Bio

I received my M.S. degree in Mechanical and Electrical Engineering from Central South University (CSU) in 2022 and my B.E. degree in Mechanical Design, Manufacture and Automation from CSU in 2019. I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. degree in Information and Communication Engineering at the National University of Defense Technology (NUDT). My research interests focus on event-based vision and computational imaging, particularly on neuromorphic camera image processing and event-based object detection.
